Landmark Types

Tail vertex (Type I) — stylus entry
Left vertex (Type II) — head left
Right vertex (Type II) — head right
Depth point (Type III) — interior
Shapes follow Bookstein landmark classification adapted for cuneiform paleography.

Protocol

Each wedge impression is annotated with 4 fixed landmarks and 3 curves:

  • tail_vertex — where the stylus shaft enters
  • left_vertex — left extent of wedge head
  • right_vertex — right extent of wedge head
  • depth_point — interior depth reference

Curves trace the outer edges of each wedge between vertex pairs.
