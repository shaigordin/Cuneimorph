Vertices (Ecken)

pₗ Left vertex (linke Ecke) — head left
pᵣ Right vertex (rechte Ecke) — head right
pₜ Depth point (Tiefpunkt) — interior

Protocol

Geometric model follows Cammarosano et al. (2014). Each wedge is annotated with:

  • Minimal: 4 vertices + 3 outer edges (Aussenkanten)
  • Standard: + 3 inner edges (Innenkanten)
  • Full: + computed angles & face areas

Curves trace edges between vertex pairs. Landmark types follow Bookstein classification.

Ref: Cammarosano, Müller, Fisseler & Weichert (2014) "Schriftmetrologie des Keils"
